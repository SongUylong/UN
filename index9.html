Who We Are

Global SDGs & Leadership Development Center (GSLDC)

Founded in November 2024, the Center works in strategic cooperation with UNITAR to translate the Sustainable Development Goals into practical action. We empower governments, institutions, corporates, and youth to build future-ready capabilities.

VISION

A generation of visionary leaders uniting hearts, minds, and actions for a sustainable world.

MISSION

Empowering changemakers through education, innovation, and collaboration.

Our Approach

Three Pillars of Impact

Cultivating Future Leaders

We nurture top talents through skills development. By equipping youth with critical thinking, we enable them to drive positive change.

Cross-Sector Collaboration

We foster strategic partnerships among governments and businesses to create authoritative policy support and impactful solutions.

Accelerating Innovation

We promote global innovative leadership by providing a dynamic ecosystem where entrepreneurship and tech solutions flourish.
