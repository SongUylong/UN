About The Center

Bridging Learning, Leadership & Innovation

The Global SDGs and Leadership Development Center (GSLDC) creates a bridge between UNITAR and regional youth. We translate the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) into practical action through training, applied research, and innovation.

Our Vision

"A generation of visionary leaders uniting hearts, minds, and actions for a sustainable world."

Our Mission

Empowering changemakers through education, innovation, and collaboration to enable real-world solutions.

Strategic Cooperation

UNITAR & Singapore

We connect governments, businesses, and young leaders. By fostering authoritative policy support and knowledge-sharing platforms, we create impactful solutions.

Our Strategy

Strategic
Pillars.

Our framework for impact is built on three core pillars designed to accelerate the 2030 Agenda.

01

Cultivating Future Leaders

We nurture emerging leaders and top talents through education and skills development. By equipping youth with critical thinking and sustainability knowledge, we enable them to drive positive change.

02

Cross-Sector Collaboration

Fostering strategic partnerships among governments, businesses, and organizations. We use authoritative policy support to address pressing social and environmental challenges.

03

Accelerating Social Innovation

Promoting global innovative leadership by providing a dynamic ecosystem where groundbreaking ideas, entrepreneurship, and technology-driven solutions flourish.
