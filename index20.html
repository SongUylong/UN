Vision & Mission

To create a world where international cooperation solves the root causes of conflict and poverty.

Through our distinct pillars of Security, Development, and Human Rights, we deploy experts and resources to the most vulnerable regions. We believe in a bottom-up approach, listening to communities to inform high-level policy.

  • Sustainable Development Goals implementation
  • Crisis Response & Humanitarian Aid

GLOBAL ALLIANCE

The Global Alliance is an international organization founded in 1945. It is currently made up of 193 Member States. The mission and work of the Alliance are guided by the purposes and principles contained in its founding Charter.
